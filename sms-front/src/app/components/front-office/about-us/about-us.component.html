OUR STORY


After 7 years of experience in the finest Italian cuisines, Chef Antonio comes to Romania and wants to open a pizzeria.

"Pizzeta" is the name of the italian restaurant and it opened its doors in October 2018, bringing authentic (and delicious!) Naples-style pizzas tothe North-Eastern region of Romania, in 5 major cities of Moldova: Iasi, Bacau, Botosani, Suceava and Vaslui.

At Pizzetta we want to make the best pizza EVER! To make that happen we take our research very seriously!
We have hunted high and low for the perfect blend of flour to make sure the dough is just how it should be.
We then found the sweetest, ripest Italian tomatoes from the rich earth of Naples.
These are picked especially for us and are the base for our secret recipe sauce.
